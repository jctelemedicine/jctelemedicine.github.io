Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is telemedicine, and why is it important?

Telemedicine is an emerging area of practice that allows medical professionals to evaluate, diagnose, and even treat patients remotely. As we move into an increasingly digital world, telemedicine is becoming incredibly important. Businesses, consumers, and practitioners must keep up or risk falling behind—and JC Telemedicine can help.

How can JC Telemedicine help my business grow and succeed?

We understand telemedicine, but that's not all. We are entrepreneurs, technologists, salespeople, and, above all, consumers. We are intimately familiar with the needs of businesses both small and large, and we know what it takes to get you from Point A to Point B because we've done it many times. Our goal is to accelerate digital transformation across all of healthcare. Whether you want to scale your operation, integrate cutting-edge technologies like blockchain and AI, or just want to learn about telemedicine, we have the expertise to help.

How has COVID-19 changed the telehealth landscape?

COVID-19 has done a lot to accelerate telehealth and the adoption of new (sometimes untested) digital technologies. While largely positive, one unintended consequence has been care providers of every size charging into this new digital landscape without much of a safety net. As we navigate a post-COVID reality, a primary component of our mission is to help organizations avoid making costly mistakes.

I'm a small/medium/large company. Can JC Telemedicine still benefit me?

Absolutely. We've worked with some of the largest companies in the world, as well as single-person operations—all in the same week. We understand all aspects of health & wellness businesses at every growth stage. Whatever your needs, we can help.

As a consumer/pharmacy/healthcare provider, how do I know whether telemedicine is right for me?

Ultimately, that depends a lot on how you're approaching telemedicine, but the blanket answer is: telemedicine and other digital health technologies can benefit almost everyone. Consumers will enjoy the flexibility and convenience; businesses and healthcare organizations will appreciate the efficiency, efficacy, and economics that underpin a transition into the digital world. Our largest clients are pharmacies that are ready to scale with direct-to-consumer offerings, businesses who are looking to leverage the power of digital health for their own products or investments, and healthcare practices who want to make sure they're providing the best, most seamless, and most modern experience for their consumers. Whoever you are, JC Telemedicine can help.
